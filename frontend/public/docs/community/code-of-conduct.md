# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces, including:

- GitHub repositories and discussions
- Telegram channels
- Twitter/X interactions
- YouTube comments
- Community events (online and offline)
- Email communications
- Any other official community channels

It also applies when representing the community in public spaces, such as using an official email address, posting via official social media accounts, or acting as a representative at events.

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it:

**Email**: conduct@reputationdao.dev

**Include:**
- Your contact information
- Names of people involved
- Description of the incident
- Date and time
- Location (channel, event, etc.)
- Any supporting evidence (screenshots, logs)
- Whether you want to remain anonymous

**Confidentiality**: All reports will be handled with discretion and confidentiality.

### Response Process

1. **Acknowledgment**: We'll acknowledge receipt within 24 hours

2. **Investigation**: We'll review the report and gather additional information if needed

3. **Decision**: We'll determine appropriate action based on the severity and context

4. **Action**: We'll take appropriate enforcement action

5. **Follow-up**: We'll follow up with the reporter on the outcome

### Enforcement Actions

Depending on the severity and context, actions may include:

#### 1. Warning

**Scope**: Minor first-time violations

**Action**: Private written warning explaining the violation and expected behavior change

**Duration**: N/A

#### 2. Temporary Ban

**Scope**: Repeated minor violations or single moderate violation

**Action**: Temporary removal from community spaces

**Duration**: 7-30 days depending on severity

**Conditions**: May require acknowledgment of violation and commitment to change

#### 3. Permanent Ban

**Scope**: Severe violations or repeated violations after warnings

**Action**: Permanent removal from all community spaces

**Duration**: Indefinite

**Appeal**: May appeal after 6 months with demonstrated behavior change

#### 4. Immediate Ban

**Scope**: Severe violations (threats, doxxing, harassment)

**Action**: Immediate permanent removal without warning

**Duration**: Indefinite

**Appeal**: Case-by-case basis

## Community Guidelines

### Communication

**Be Clear and Constructive**
- State your point clearly
- Provide context and examples
- Offer solutions, not just criticism
- Assume good intentions

**Stay On Topic**
- Keep discussions relevant
- Use appropriate channels
- Don't derail conversations
- Create new threads for new topics

**Respect Time**
- Search before asking
- Read documentation first
- Don't spam or cross-post
- Be patient waiting for responses

### Technical Discussions

**Be Helpful**
- Share knowledge generously
- Explain your reasoning
- Link to relevant resources
- Help others learn

**Be Humble**
- Acknowledge when you're wrong
- Learn from mistakes
- Ask questions when unsure
- Respect different approaches

**Be Thorough**
- Provide complete information
- Include error messages and logs
- Share reproduction steps
- Test before suggesting solutions

### Contributions

**Follow Guidelines**
- Read contribution guidelines
- Follow code style
- Write clear commit messages
- Add tests and documentation

**Respect Process**
- Open issues before PRs
- Respond to review feedback
- Be patient with reviews
- Accept decisions gracefully

**Give Credit**
- Acknowledge others' work
- Link to sources
- Don't plagiarize
- Share recognition

## Diversity & Inclusion

We are committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive community:

**Accessibility**
- Use clear, simple language
- Provide alt text for images
- Consider different time zones
- Accommodate different abilities

**Representation**
- Amplify underrepresented voices
- Challenge bias and stereotypes
- Create inclusive spaces
- Celebrate diversity

**Education**
- Learn about different perspectives
- Ask questions respectfully
- Admit when you don't know
- Grow from feedback

## Leadership Responsibilities

Community leaders are responsible for:

- Clarifying and enforcing standards
- Taking appropriate corrective action
- Removing inappropriate content
- Communicating reasons for moderation decisions
- Leading by example

Leaders who don't follow or enforce the Code of Conduct may face temporary or permanent repercussions.
